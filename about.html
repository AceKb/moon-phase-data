Welcome to AstroDash!

AstroDash is a data dashboard designed to provide insightful information about moon phases and temperature data. Here, you can explore various moon phases, their corresponding temperatures, and visualize this data through interactive charts.

Features:

  • Interactive charts for temperature and moon phases.
  • Filter data by date and moon phase.
  • Detailed information about specific moon phases.
